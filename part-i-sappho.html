part i: disciples of sappho

The Isle of Lesbos: The influence of Sappho on lesbian and queer history for well over two thousand years is undeniable and yet we can be sure of almost nothing about the woman herself. This is an exploration of Sappho’s influence on queer literature through the ages. I’m curious to read all the different interpretations of her life and loves. What kind of picture can be drawn of Sappho by piecing together some of the art that’s sprinkled through history? The only thing I’m certain of is that it will be entirely a fiction.
